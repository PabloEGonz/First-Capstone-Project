Featured Artists

Angèle

Belgian singer, songwriter, and producer.

Angèle approaches contemporary dance-pop with a classic French pop bent. She went all the way to number one in France and Belgium with her debut album, 2018's Brol.

Aya Nakamura

French-Malian singer, and songwriter.

After a remarkable debut on social networks and numerous collaborations, she made a breakthrough with the release of her first album, which was certified platinum after just over two years.

Stromae

Belgian singer, rapper, songwriter and producer.

He is mostly known for his music blending hip hop and electronic music. Stromae came to wide public attention in 2009 with his song Alors on danse.

Video Club

French musical project formed in Nantes

They became popular for the song Amour Plastique, which was released in the September of 2018, which has accumulated more than 107 million views on YouTube as of March 2023.

Indila

French singer and songwriter.

She collaborated with many musicians on vocals and songwriting prior to releasing her first single, Dernière danse , in December 2013, which reached SNEP Top 2 in France.

Pomme

French singer, songwriter, and musician.

Her debut studio album, À peu près (2017), received critical acclaim for its mixture of pop and folk music and entered the French album charts at number 91.
