Vive la Musique: Celebrating the Sounds of France - Festival 2023

Welcome to our annual music festival featuring talented artists from French-speaking countries, taking place in Paris, France. Get ready for an unforgettable celebration of culture, diversity, and music!

This year's lineup includes a mix of established and emerging artists, spanning a variety of genres from pop to hip-hop to traditional folk music. With performances spread out across multiple stages, there will be something for everyone to enjoy. So come join us and experience the vibrant energy of the French-speaking music scene!

If you have further questions, please contact us by email: info@vivelamusique.fr.com

Vive la Musique's Logo

Vive la Musique's logo, with its striking eye and accompanying text, symbolizes the idea of discovering diverse cultures through the medium of music festivals. It invites us to open our eyes and immerse ourselves in the eclectic and vibrant world of festival culture, where we can experience the richness and diversity of different cultures and traditions.
